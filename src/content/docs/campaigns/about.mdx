This is a community reference for **the Infantaverse**, the shared setting spanning four
canon campaigns. It is generated from the campaigns' own materials.

## The sourcing rule

Every lore article is drawn **only** from the creator's source material (session notes,
journals, handouts, archives, character sheets, maps) or the creator's explicit direction.
Nothing is invented or "interpreted" into canon. Each article lists its `sources`. Where the
record is thin or missing, the page shows a **needs-source** banner instead of being filled
with plausible guesswork.

## Reading "Oblivia"

In-world, the event known as the **End of the Infanta** erased knowledge from the world.
