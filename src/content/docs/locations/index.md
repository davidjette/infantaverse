The places of the chronicle — from a single island in the Moonshae to the prison-womb at the
center of everything.

- **[Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/)** — the buried crystal city, once the capital of the world.
- **[Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/)** — the elves' home island, birthplace of the first Infanta.
- **[Sigil](/locations/sigil/)** — the City of Doors, nexus of the planes and seat of the Company.
- **[The Egg](/locations/the-egg/)** — the prison-womb of the sleeping god, where all Times converge.
