**The Egg** is the womb-prison in which the fetal god [Leviathan](/creatures/the-leviathan/) lies
folded — also the **Black Omen**, the **God Egg**, and, falling toward the world as a pristine
black sphere, the **Evening Star**. It is reached only through **the Crack**, a wound beneath
[Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/).

## Inside

Within, the laws of the world fail. Geometry confounds — "distance halves within three hundred
feet of the god and halves again within one hundred." A sea of acid "dissolves all but the god,"
and there is "no teleportation, plane shift, or astral escape from the Egg while the god lives."
The shell can be parted only by certain magics, and only the god himself — reborn through a
fragment of his child Zoth — can break it.

## Where all Times meet

<Oblivia label="The convergence">
June's revelation gives the Egg its strangest property: "inside the Egg all Times converge." The
party's souls, scattered across the ages, are told they will "meet each other there," and must
"bring every force they can from whenever they are to defeat Him." The **[End of the
Infanta](/events/end-of-the-infanta/)** plays out inside it — every ally of every age filing into
the acid meat of the wound, the [Lady of Pain](/characters/marcus/) and Marcus arriving with the
severed head of Asmodeus, and the final Wish that, touching the god with an
[Obliviator](/items/the-obliviator/), unwrites everything the Egg contained.
</Oblivia>
