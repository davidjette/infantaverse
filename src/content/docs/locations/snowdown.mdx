**Snowdown** — anciently **Wain Gallidan** — is "the elves' own home island and the seat of the
Cult of [Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/), the [Lusmundii](/factions/the-lusmundii/)." It is where
"the first [Infanta](/concepts/the-infanta/) was born," the seat of [House
Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/), and the island whose Snowy Mountain hides the buried city
of [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/).

## Places of the island

The chronicle's first arc crosses it: **Leftfork Farm**, worked by enslaved elves under the Shors;
the goblin warren of **Heartstone Cave**; the burning plantation of **Grenphal**; **Bask Falls
Barrow**, the ancestral tomb of House Gallidann where [Star Quendi](/characters/star-quendi/) lies;
and the walled town of **Harloch**, whose Old Temple the heroes buy to found **Temple Holdings,
LLC**. At its heart stands the **Gildergreen**, the sacred tree where the Infanta is meant to grow
up "a child rather than a god." By the [Age of the Infanta](/events/age-of-the-infanta/), Snowdown
and its moonstone are the prize the [Company](/factions/sword-coast-trading-company/) and the
reborn goddess fight to control.
