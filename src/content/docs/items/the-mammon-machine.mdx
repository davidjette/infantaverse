The **Mammon Machine** is "the greatest treasure of [doomed Netheril]" — an "energy collection and
amplification device" with which the Netherese "claimed to have slain the god of death and captured
his essence in the machine's heart." It is the vessel of **Zoth**, and the Netherese broadcast
knowledge of their captured "god-monster, Cthulhu," proclaiming "that man had overcome the need for
Gods."

## The engine of an empire

The Machine lets the Netherese "strum the Weave like a guitar," powering the **Flux** that drives
every mythallar and flying city of the empire — drawing the magic of the dead, through leylines
that emanate from it and the [Obliviator](/items/the-obliviator/). It was lost in the calamity
that destroyed Netheril and now lies "several miles deep in the Anauroch Desert, subsumed in
igneous rock." [Wainwright St. Cloud](/characters/wainwright-st-cloud/) hunts it across three
candidate sites, then goes silent in pursuit of it.

## The Apotheosis

<Oblivia label="What the Machine is really for">
The Company's true design is the **Apotheosis of Man**: to bring the Mammon Machine to the **Black
Omen**, the [Egg](/locations/), and "create a New Star… a moon of Pure Magic." What the Netherese
queens believe is salvation is annihilation — channeling the souls of the dead into the Egg until
"[Cthulhu](/creatures/the-leviathan/) remerges with the remnants of Zoth, and Time and
Consciousness itself give way to total oblivion." St. Cloud deceived them into bringing "all the
magic of their world to bear against their own existence."
</Oblivia>
