The **Anachron** is the Company's most dangerous engine: an elemental drive that "generate[s]
arcane gravity fields," and, amplified by a Power [moonstone](/concepts/moonstone/), "warp[s] two
spaces closer together across a single vector (a temporary portal) which allows rapid travel across
planes." From it the Company's R&D "devised the **Arcane Core** which powers the dozen or so Company
airships that were ever built" — including the [Company](/factions/sword-coast-trading-company/)
flagship the heroes capture and rename the **Arielle**.

## Time, and Perfect Dark

<Oblivia label="The price of warping time">
The legends say the Anachron can warp *time* as it warps space — but the Company concluded the feat
"would require a store of information that is unfathomable… a perfect moonstone so far beyond its
half life that it was twice as old as the oldest moonstone on Faerûn," the hypothetical Phase V
they called **Perfect Dark**. Believing it impossible, Special Projects re-sequestered the device.

It is not impossible. Powered through the time-goddess **June**, the Anachron carries the heroes
into the deep past at **[Starfall](/events/starfall/)**, where [Wainwright St.
Cloud](/characters/wainwright-st-cloud/) scrambles its core and flees into a time portal — flinging
the party apart across the ages.
</Oblivia>
