**Silverbane** is the [moonstone](/concepts/moonstone/) blade of [House
Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/) — a sentient, speaking sword that remembers what the world
forgot. First found dormant as **Feybane** in the deep hold of the *Evening Star*, interred with
the *baelnorn* Daenan, it passes to [Drefan](/characters/), sanctified to him by the spirit of
[Star Quendi](/characters/star-quendi/).

## The blades that remember

The moonblades are **five siblings** — Silverbane, **Ebonbane**, **Ironbane** (Excalibur),
**Glassbane**, and the lost **Copperbane** — each the soul of an elven people, forged from the
fallen moon. In the deep past of the [Time of Troubles](/events/time-of-troubles/), away from the
Oblivia that erased that age, they begin to **remember**:

> I remember these times much better now… much of this age was erased from history, which for you
> is written record but for me, is empty spaces spoiling my persistent memory.

<Oblivia label="What Silverbane recalled">
Silverbane's recovered memory is one of the chronicle's keystones — Elistrae fused to a mortal
girl, her forbidden love for an avatar of a dwarven god, [Eldamir](/factions/house-gallidann/) and
[Calix](/characters/calix/) "moments away from drowning their entire landing camp in the Sea of
Swords" before the goddess stayed their hand and gave them the [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/).
The blade also carries the house's darkest secret: that after the Forgetting, it served Eldamir
through the genocide of the dwarves.
</Oblivia>

By [Arc VII — Leviathan](/events/leviathan/), the peoples of every age hail the heroes as the
rightful bearers of all five blades — "you bear all the Silverbanes across all time."
