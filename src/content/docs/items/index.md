The relics and engines of the chronicle — from a moon-forged blade that remembers to the machines
that erase and devour.

## The Infernal Machines

- **[The Obliviator](/items/the-obliviator/)** — the engine of forgetting, source of the Oblivia.
- **[The Lexicon](/items/the-lexicon/)** — the mind of Motherstone, grown in a perfect moonstone.
- **[The Mammon Machine](/items/the-mammon-machine/)** — Netheril's vessel of the captured god of death.
- **[The Anachron](/items/the-anachron/)** — the drive that warps space, and with the right stone, time.

## Relics

- **[Silverbane](/items/silverbane/)** — the moonblade of House Gallidann, eldest of five sibling swords.
