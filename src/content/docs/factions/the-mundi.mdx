The **Mundi** are the white moon-elves of [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/) — "the protectors
of the crystal gardens, this slice of the moon," who "live in harmony with the water, stone and
air, and… harvest the breath of the mountain." They dwell inside the city's great oaks, ruled by
their king **Chad**, "watcher of the stones and the destroyer of Drek."

## The diadem and the prophecy

The Mundi exist to hold a single object against a single prophecy. As Chad explains, a "child god
granted this place to our people [but] she was betrayed by the ancestors of the [Drek](/factions/the-drek/)
who invited Ayror into the city." So long as the Mundi keep their protective **diadem**, "their
evil magics cannot take hold" — guarding against the prophecy that warns "to find the seven princes
betrayed, and don the diadem on the empty throne." They mistake [Drefan](/characters/), bearer of
[Silverbane](/items/), for a returning king, and revere the moonblade as their rightful lord.
