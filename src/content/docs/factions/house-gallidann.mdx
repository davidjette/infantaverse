**House Gallidann** is the elven house bound to the [Infanta](/characters/elistrae/) and the wilds
of the Moonshae. Founded by **Eldamir**, who "came with a wave of elves from the Feywild into the
north around the time of Troubles," it raised the first Infanta [Elistrada](/characters/) through
the guardian [Star Quendi](/characters/star-quendi/), and through her son **Renos** and grandson
the *baelnorn* **Daenan** kept Snowdown for ages. Its members bear the sentient **moonblades**, and
its rune still glows on [Silverbane](/items/).

## A line of guardians

Eldamir, Star Quendi, Renos, Daenan — the house "swore [an] oath to protect the Infanta forever,"
and founded [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/) when Elistrae gave it to them. Its great enemy
was Eldamir's own brother, [Calix](/characters/calix/), who turned *baelnorn* and allied with
**Queen Maab** against his kin.

## A terrible history

<Oblivia label="The genocide the moonblade remembers">
After the Forgetting, the house's grief curdled into atrocity. Silverbane confesses that Eldamir
"blamed the dwarves and pledged to ride them from the Moonshae… led a war against the remaining
dwarven armies… [and then] a genocide, capturing entire populations and leading them still alive
into their tombs." Only afterward did she discover Calix's plot and slay him, dying by his hand.
The blood seal beneath Motherstone, which demands the blood of a dwarf to open, is a monument to
this crime.
</Oblivia>

In the rewritten [Time of Troubles](/events/time-of-troubles/), the house's radicalized heir
**Ilana** leads it during Drefan's absence, strained between its oath to the Infanta and its long
alliance with the Forsworn and Calix.
