The houses, companies, cults, and peoples whose ambitions drive the chronicle.

- **[House Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/)** — the elven house sworn to guard the Infanta, keepers of the moonblades.
- **[The Sword Coast Trading Company](/factions/sword-coast-trading-company/)** — the merchant empire run by changelings in service of one immortal man.
- **[The Lusmundii](/factions/the-lusmundii/)** — the druidic Cult of the Infanta, "Light of the World."
- **[The Mundi](/factions/the-mundi/)** — the white moon-elves who keep the crystal gardens of Motherstone.
- **[The Drek](/factions/the-drek/)** — the spider-worshipping folk of Motherstone's drowning depths.
