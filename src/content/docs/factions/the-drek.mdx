The **Drek** are the folk of [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/)'s depths — "the loathsome
Drek, who carved the tunnels and pits into this rock," in the contemptuous words of the
[Mundi](/factions/the-mundi/) above them. They toil "for the love of their wretched spider god
**Ayror**," and live in a vast slum, the **Drembles**, that lines the wall of the old crystal mine
and descends hundreds of feet to a pool of rising, fetid water.

## Caught between

The Drek are dying — "trapped between the rising waters and the threats outside," the flood
swallowing their city from below while the awakened machines hunt them from the deep. Their own
legend reverses the Mundi's: that the Mundi "turned on the Drek and slaughtered them by the
thousands," and that **Ayror**, "who was once a great god of light and knowledge," cursed them all.
Both peoples are heirs of a betrayal neither can remember — and the god they fear, **Ayror**, is in
truth no god at all, but the **error** state of the broken [Lexicon](/items/the-lexicon/).
