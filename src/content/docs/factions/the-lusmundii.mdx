The **Lusmundii** — "Light of the World" — are the druidic cult formed around the birth of the
reborn [Infanta](/characters/elistrae/). Elves and humans together, they "believe the Infanta will
turn the tide of history against all forms of civilization and allow nature to reclaim Faerûn,"
seeking "to end the three-way conflict between the Sylvan Elves, the human settlers, and the
renegade Forsworn by uniting them all behind a fanatical devotion to nature."

## The child beneath the tree

Led by the elven chief **Untari**, the cult's charge — given by the spirit of
[Star Quendi](/characters/star-quendi/) — is to bring the Infanta to the **Gildergreen**, the
sacred tree at the heart of [Snowdown](/locations/), and let her "grow up a child rather than a
god." Their members appear throughout the chronicle: the rogue **Raylin** who tries to free the
infant aboard the *Evening Star*, the priestesses who chant the sacred mythal of protection, and
the community that becomes the refuge bought and named **Temple Holdings, LLC**.
