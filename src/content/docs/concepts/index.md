The ideas that hold the setting together — the metaphysics beneath the chronicle.

- **[The Infanta](/concepts/the-infanta/)** — a god reborn into a mortal child, the phenomenon that names the age.
- **[Moonstone](/concepts/moonstone/)** — shards of the fallen moon that store mind, memory, and time itself.
