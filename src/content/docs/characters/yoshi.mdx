**Yoshi** is the copper dragonborn who washed up on a faraway island to be raised by human farmers,
never knowing he was the survivor of a slaughtered honor guard bred for the Infanta herself. His arc
is the chronicle's quietest tragedy and its warmest homecoming: the orphan who finds out, at last,
where he came from.

## The trusting hero

Among the protagonists named on [Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/) in [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/) —
"**Yoshi** and his companion **Denver**" — he is also the source of the party's first great loss.
Returning from victory, "the party finds the [Infanta](/concepts/the-infanta/) gone, carried off by
the wet-nurse Yoshi had trusted with her," sending the heroes on the long chase to Bask Falls.

## Son of the dragon

<Oblivia label="Where Yoshi came from">
In [Arc II](/events/motherstone/), Yoshi follows a hooded Drek priest "with a golden snout like his
own" to a flooded temple, and an ancient Copper Dragonborn tells him the truth: the roost of
Motherstone belonged to a living god — "you and I would be more precise to call him — *father*." The
dragon was [Tylerjinex](/characters/tylerjinex/), who pledged fealty to the Infanta and conceived
**seven eggs** as her honor guard. The traitor [Calix](/characters/calix/) banished the dragon and
had the hatchlings slain in their sleep by the agents of [House Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/).
Two eggs were stolen and discarded into a seam in the rock — and against all odds survived. One of
them was Yoshi.
</Oblivia>

The elder gives him the honor-guard's weapon, a blade that draws its power "from the arcane magics of
the Earth itself" and burns with moonlight.
