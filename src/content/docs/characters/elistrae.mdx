**Elistrae** — the Dark Maiden — is the cast-out moon-goddess at the center of the chronicle: "an
Elven god of chaos, beauty, hunting, moonlight and song… the god of all dark elves who have left
the Underdark to come to live on the surface." Sister of **Lolth**, daughter of **Sylvanus**, she
is the deity who hides inside mortal children to be born into the world as the **Infanta**.

## The first Infanta

In the age of the Heavenfall, when the gods were sealed away from the world, "the cast-out
moon-goddess Elistrae entered a dying human child through its mother's prayers, and grew into the
living goddess-queen [Elistrada](/characters/)." Raised by her guardian [Star
Quendi](/characters/) of [House Gallidann](/factions/), Elistrada ruled an elven empire in the
Moonshae — remembered in later, mournful ages as "the throne of tears."

## The Infanta reborn

The infant the heroes guard across [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/) is Elistrae reborn — "a
changeling child… worshipped by a druidic cult who believe the child will turn the tide of history
against all kingdoms, laws, and cultivation, and return the world to nature." The cost of harming
her is the **Cries of Elistrae**, a scream that "shake[s] the Earth, wracking the entire island
and bringing the walls of Harloch to the ground." Star Quendi's charge to her protectors sets the
chronicle's course:

> But she mustn't be raised as a god. She must be a child, living beneath the Gildergreen.

## The Queen of Motherstone

By the [Age of the Infanta](/events/age-of-the-infanta/), ten years on, she has grown into a young
**Empress**, retaking Motherstone — "her divine form is awesome, but she is still a small human
girl." She convenes the [Council of Forgetting](/events/age-of-the-infanta/) over whether to hide
her kingdom from the world's memory. When the [Lexicon](/items/the-lexicon/) breaks, she is "cut
off from moon worshipers all across the Universe," and falls into grief, invading her champions'
dreams in her desperation to repair it and complete Brightmantle's vision of a "City of Infanta, a
holy arc through the Time of Troubles."

<Oblivia label="What Elistrae is, beneath the goddess">
Per the revelation at the end of time, the gods of the realm — Elistrae among them — are "the split
soul of the Star Child, whose name was Zoth-Ommog," who "left [their] father somewhere deep in the
dark, and fled him across all of time." Elistrae is not merely a moon-goddess but a fragment of
divided memory, hiding from the blind devourer [Cthulhu](/creatures/the-leviathan/) in the one
place he cannot see: a self that happens *in Time*.
</Oblivia>

## Relationships

Reborn line of the goddess; ward of **Star Quendi** and **Untari**; beloved of an avatar of a
dwarven god (Brightmantle) — "a genuine blasphemy" that founded Motherstone; guarded by the
**Lusmundii** and the moonblade **Silverbane**.
