**Tylerjinex** is the great **copper dragon** who roosted on Snowy Mountain before the Infantas
came — "the roost of a great being. Some would say a living god. But you and I would be more precise
to call him — father." Seeing goodness in [Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/), he pledged his fealty
and conceived **seven eggs** to be her honor-guard, raised by the furnace of the Underforge.

## Yoshi's father

<Oblivia label="The murdered brood, and the survivor">
[Calix](/characters/calix/) "beguiled Tylerjinex and banished him to a dark abyss," and the
hatchlings "were slain in their sleep — by the agents of [House Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/),
who could not stand to see anyone but elves at the side of the Infanta." Two stolen eggs survived,
discarded into a seam in the rock; one of them was **Yoshi**, washed up on a faraway island, "the
only Copper Dragonborn he would ever meet" until the chronicle reunited them.
</Oblivia>

## The man behind the dragon

In the deep history of the [Time of Troubles](/events/time-of-troubles/), Tylerjinex is also
**Peter Glans**, husband of the elf-queen **Alen Tudix** of Myth Drannor, who hid the moonblade
**Copperbane** in the Penteract. Heartbroken at her death, he names [Kara](/characters/) his heir.
By [Arc VII — Leviathan](/events/leviathan/), he gives the chronicle's most complete pledge — naming
Kara "the mother of my Egg" — and stays behind to guard the egg they made together so she can
fulfill her mission.
