**Star Quendi** was the daughter of **Eldamir** and heir to [House Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/),
who became guardian to the first Infanta. When the child became "a sentient god in a mortal body" —
[Elistrada](/characters/), the goddess [Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/) reborn — Quendi "raised
her, ruled in her name," and rose to the highest order of elven nobility, **Star**. She "now sits
immortal in the throne-forests of **Arboria**, in the deepest heart of the Feywild."

## The charge at Bask Falls

Quendi built the barrow at **Bask Falls** for her mother, and it is there — in [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/) —
that her spectral echo, nearly bound by the Forsworn into a new child, instead blesses the heroes
and names their purpose:

> Who are you, champions? Who are you, champions of Elistrae and the Moonshae Isles? Tell me your
> names so I may sing songs of you among the gildergreens in throne forests of Arboria… But she
> mustn't be raised as a god. She must be a child, living beneath the Gildergreen.

She grants [Drefan](/characters/) the right to wield [Silverbane](/items/) if he pledges to live
and die for her house, and her boon — the immediate trust of any child — to the whole party.
