**Kara** is the merchant who became the chronicle's great spellcaster — the wizard-merchant who
walks into [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/) chasing a clue and walks out, arcs later, holding
the power to rewrite reality. More than any other hero she is the engine of the party's victories:
the one who finds the way in, fixes the bearing, and pays the price.

## The merchant-mage

Named among the protagonists on [Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/) in [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/),
Kara follows the half-elf apothecary Fhastine's moon-marked clue to a mine beneath Snowy Mountain
and, with [Brother Magnus](/characters/brother-magnus/), breaks into the buried moonstone machine
that opens [Arc II — Motherstone](/events/motherstone/). It is Kara who keeps the party on the
Infanta's trail: when the child is carried off, she "fixes the child's bearing with the **Quicksilver
Sextant**," the dark-elf navigator's relic that "reveals the direction of any known object, place or
person."

## Kara's Wish

The arc the heroes call **Kara's Wish** turns on a single sentence. Cornered by the forces of
**Talona** in [Arc VI](/events/time-of-troubles/), Kara spends a *Wish* — that they *had* moved the
Moon in the past — and reality reshapes itself around the party: the citadel transforms, a hostile
god becomes an ally, and a history in which the deflection succeeded writes itself into the walls.
A world remade by one caster's sentence is the truest measure of what she has become.

## The green-eyed sorceress

<Oblivia label="The portrait in the Old Man's House">
In St. Cloud's hidden lair in [Arc IV](/events/the-infernal-machines/), a great portrait overlooks
the studio of half-built machines: "a sorceress in flight, the moon exploding into blue shards above
her, her face Kara's — but her eyes green." The immortal has been painting her across the ages, one
recurring soul among the bent-timeline heroes he has watched "over and over again."
</Oblivia>

By the muster of [Arc VII](/events/leviathan/), Kara's part in the chronicle is both cosmic and
intimate: she and the copper dragon [Tylerjinex](/characters/tylerjinex/) — in his mortal guise as
Peter Glans — have "made an egg of their own," and he stays behind to guard it so she can carry the
final mission into the [Egg](/locations/the-egg/) itself.
