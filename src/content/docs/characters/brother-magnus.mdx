**Brother Magnus** is the dwarf at the party's spiritual center: a cleric of **Brightmantle**, the God
of Discovery and Invention, whose own fortunes rise and fall with his god's. When the holy magic of
the world begins to fail, no hero feels it more personally than Magnus.

## The dwarf cleric

Named among the protagonists on [Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/) in [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/),
"the dwarf **Brother Magnus**" is the companion who breaks into the Snowy Mountain machine alongside
[Kara](/characters/kara/) to open [Arc II — Motherstone](/events/motherstone/). His booming at the
court of the [Mundi](/factions/the-mundi/) king both cows and antagonizes them, and his reputation
spreads: by [Arc III](/events/age-of-the-infanta/), the prince [Zanzibar](/characters/zanzibar/) is
dispatched from Cormyr "to ask Brother Magnus about his vision and the fading of the gods."

## The death of a god

<Oblivia label="Brightmantle's last gift">
In [Arc VII](/events/leviathan/), **Brightmantle** — "the God of Discovery and Invention and Brother
Magnus's patron" — is dying, and "Magnus's own power fades to a guttering remnant as his god slips
away." But the god leaves enough of himself in his final act, channeled through Magnus, "that the
dwarf recovers his full power and can empower the faithful around him — the god's last gift before the
grave." Magnus becomes the conduit by which a dying god arms the muster against the
[Leviathan](/creatures/the-leviathan/).
</Oblivia>
