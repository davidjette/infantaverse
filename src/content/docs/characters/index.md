The people of the Infantaverse — gods reborn as children, the immortals who hunt them, and the
band of strangers who became the chronicle's heroes.

## Gods & Infantas

- **[Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/)** — the cast-out moon-goddess, reborn again and again as the Infanta.
- **[Star Quendi](/characters/star-quendi/)** — guardian of the first Infanta, ascended to Arboria.
- **[Marcus](/characters/marcus/)** — the boy who is the Raven Queen, the Infanta of Death.

## Antagonists

- **[Calix the Betrayer](/characters/calix/)** — the elf-lich who authored the Forgetting.
- **[Wainwright St. Cloud](/characters/wainwright-st-cloud/)** — the immortal behind the Company, vessel of the Leviathan.

## Allies & chroniclers

- **[Tylerjinex](/characters/tylerjinex/)** — the copper dragon of Motherstone, father of the dragonborn.
- **[Mara Mageblood](/characters/mara-mageblood/)** — the explorer who read the cursed history and went blind for it.
