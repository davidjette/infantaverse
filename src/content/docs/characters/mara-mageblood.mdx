**Mara Mageblood** came this way a century before the heroes. An elf scholar who chased the
forgotten history of [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/), she read a cursed Orcish tome, *The
Betrayal of Motherstone*, and paid for it — going blind in one eye, then dying in a makeshift camp
deep in the Snowy Mountain machine. Her last letter, found by [Kara](/characters/), points the way:

> I have found its belly, but in breaking it I have undone myself… I've written it all down for
> you, but I fear you won't be able to read it for many many centuries. If you find me, please,
> push on and find the mother stone.

## The journal

<Oblivia label="The lie at the heart of history">
[Mara Mageblood's Journal](/items/) is the chronicle's clearest in-world testimony, opening with
the line the whole setting turns on — *"There is a lie at the heart of our history, one that the
entire world has forgotten"* — and recording her mother's lullaby, which encodes the entire secret
of [Calix the Betrayer](/characters/calix/) and the Forgetting. She left it hidden with her wedding
dress for her daughter **Fhastine**, fearing it would afflict her as it had afflicted Mara.
</Oblivia>
