**Drefan** is the elf at the heart of the chronicle's oldest oath: the druid sworn to
[House Gallidann](/factions/house-gallidann/) who takes up the moonblade
[Silverbane](/items/silverbane/) and carries the sword — and the sword's buried memory — through the
whole war for the Moonshae.

## The sworn elf

Named among the protagonists when the party reaches [Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/) in
[Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/), Drefan is "the elf sworn to House Gallidann who comes to bear the
moonblade **Silverbane**." Where the others are strangers to the Cult of [Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/),
he belongs to its history — and the blade he carries remembers things he never lived, waking across
the arcs to confess the genocide and betrayal at the founding of [Motherstone](/locations/motherstone/).

## Mistaken for a king

In [Arc II](/events/motherstone/), among the white moon-elves of the [Mundi](/factions/the-mundi/),
Drefan's blade marks him out: King **Chad** "mistakes Drefan, who bears the moonblade Silverbane, for
a returning king," and treats the party as long-prophesied liberators — until the court's suspicion
turns, and Chad begins quietly working to "turn Drefan against Magnus."

## The taking of the Excelsior

Drefan's signature feat comes in [Arc III](/events/age-of-the-infanta/). When the heroes raid the
Company's fleeing flagship, the **Excelsior**, "Drefan, hidden aboard as a cloud of mist, seizes the
helm." The captured ship is rechristened the **Arielle** and flown home over the mountain — the
vessel that carries the party through the rest of the chronicle.
