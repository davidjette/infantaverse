**Roland Deschain** is the chronicle's gunslinger: a human ranger and Knight of the Order who joins
the party carrying a grudge older than the quest. From his first naming, his purpose is fixed — "**Roland**,
who hunts the bloodline of St. Cloud" — and that hunt threads him into the deepest mystery of the
immortal who calls himself [Wainwright St. Cloud](/characters/wainwright-st-cloud/).

## The hunter

Named among the heroes on [Snowdown](/locations/snowdown/) in [Arc I](/events/hope-in-exile/), Roland
is set apart by his quarry rather than his faith. The Company's master has wronged his line, and
Roland tracks the St. Cloud blood across the islands and the ages — a personal vendetta that turns
out to sit very near the chronicle's cosmic one.

## The box he cannot open

<Oblivia label="The murder in the gunshop">
With the [Lexicon](/items/the-lexicon/) dark and her worshippers cut off, [Elistrae](/characters/elistrae/)
invades the heroes' dreams in [Arc IV](/events/the-infernal-machines/), and "Roland suffers worst:
she forces him again and again through the unrememberable moment in his father's gunshop, the box St.
Cloud could not open, and the murder he cannot recall — a wound the size of a thing the
[Obliviator](/items/the-obliviator/) ate." His missing memory is not ordinary trauma; it is something
the Forgetting itself devoured.
</Oblivia>
