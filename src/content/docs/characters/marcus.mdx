**Marcus** is the boy who carries the soul of the **Raven Queen** — the **Infanta of Death**. He
was hidden among the orphans of the Gravekeeper's tomb in **the Ride**, while the Horseking
**Khal Unger** and his Asmodeus-worshipping priest tortured a princess each year, trying to wake a
god that was never in her. The truth, from a child who knew him:

> But they're wrong, she isn't the devil. It's Marcus… Marcus was always the Raven Queen. He hated
> being a human locked in the tomb… And this time he had enough. He made the dead kill them and
> ran away.

## Brother to the Lady of Pain

Marcus's sister is the **Lady of Pain**, ruler of [Sigil](/locations/). Through the
[Infernal Machines](/events/the-infernal-machines/) arc he guides the heroes against
[Wainwright St. Cloud](/characters/wainwright-st-cloud/) — the immortal who "destroyed [Death] and
let Asmodeus take the land of the dead" — seeking to restore his throne and "end this Time of
Troubles." By the finale he and the Lady have slain **Asmodeus** and "returned Death to its natural
rule," arriving at the Egg to join the last battle and "smil[ing] for the first time you have ever
seen."
