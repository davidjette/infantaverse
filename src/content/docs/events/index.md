## Temple Holdings LLC — the eight arcs

1. **[Hope in Exile](/events/hope-in-exile/)** — shipwreck on the Moonshae, the infant goddess, and the long road home. *(written)*
2. **[Motherstone](/events/motherstone/)** — the buried crystal city, the Lexicon and the Obliviator, and the betrayal that erased a kingdom. *(written)*
3. **[Age of the Infanta](/events/age-of-the-infanta/)** — a reborn goddess enthroned, a moonstone empire, and the race to seize the god-children awakening across Faerûn. *(written)*
4. **[The Infernal Machines](/events/the-infernal-machines/)** — into Sigil to break the Company's machine-empire, and the truth of what Wainwright St. Cloud is. *(written)*
5. **[Starfall](/events/starfall/)** — into the deep past and the end of time, and the secret that Time itself is the Obliviator. *(written)*
6. **[Time of Troubles](/events/time-of-troubles/)** — a rewritten past, the legends of Camelot and Avalon, and the moonblades remembering. *(written)*
7. **[Leviathan](/events/leviathan/)** — a god's death, the muster of every ally across every age, and the march on Netheril. *(written)*
8. **[End of the Infanta](/events/end-of-the-infanta/)** — the last battle inside the Egg, and the Wish that becomes the Oblivia. *(written)*
